--- title: Logistic regression author: Abhijit date: '2017-05-25' slug: logistic-regression categories: - SAS ---

One of the different things in SAS is that when we’re doing logistic regression using PROC LOGISTIC, what is modeled is the opposite of what R does. If we’re modeling a binary variable Y, R models Y=1 while SAS models Y=0, which means the odds ratios are reversed.
